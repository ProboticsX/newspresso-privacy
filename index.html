Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Newspresso News ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by ProboticsX. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Newspresso News mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Phone number — used for account creation and authentication via OTP
  • Name, username, date of birth, gender — collected during onboarding to personalise your experience
  • Location (optional) — used to show locally relevant news; you may deny this permission

Information collected automatically

  • Device identifiers — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements
  • Usage data and analytics — including articles read, features used, and session information, collected via Firebase Analytics
  • Push notification token — used to deliver news alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate and maintain your account
  • Personalise your news feed and recommendations
  • Deliver push notifications about breaking news
  • Serve advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Analyse app usage to improve the product

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — database and backend infrastructure (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Google Firebase — analytics, push notifications, and authentication (firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in method (policies.google.com/privacy)

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Newspresso News is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier and other data to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
